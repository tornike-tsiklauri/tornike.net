About Me

I've had a passion for technology since I was little. My first real experience happened when I was 15 years old when I did my first reinstallation of a Windows operating system by myself. This had clearly shown me the importance of applying practical skills, paying attention to detail, and problem-solving methodically in IT. This experience changed my life and shaped me going forward. Today, I can work with computer systems comprehensively: building components, changing BIOS settings, to installing home security systems and cameras. For smartphones, I can improve the user's experience in a considerable amount by optimizing the system and installing lightweight software to make the device run extremely fast, regardless of how old it is. I pursue what I love in the belief that I can make people's lives easier; technology is an inherent part of our life.
